*** Help System ***
O: Open Manual (PDF from the web)
H: Display this Help
*** Controls ***
WASD: move person or ship around
U: switch between Ship and Person view
T: teleport
*** Inventory and Trading ***
I: pickup item into inventory or drop from inventory
SPACE: use selected item
1..9: buy from selected trade
1..9: sell inventory to person (doesn't matter which number)
*** General ***
M: toggle music
Z: toggle zoom level
P: pause/resume game
F / F11: toggle full screen
Esc: quit game
